Neon Nightmares

Banned List

General

  • No Nephandi, BSDs, Mockery Breeds, Marauders, Gorgons, Technocrats, Drones, Kami, etc
  • Hakken, Siberrakh, and other Garou regional bloodlines are not allowed
  • We do not use the Lore Ability/Background, if you want your character to know about a specific supernatural group, take the specialty in Occult

Backgrounds

  • Mage backgrounds: Back-Up, Chantry, Cult, Node, and Spies will not be approved.

Merits/Flaws

  • Child: All characters must be 18+, this is to avoid legal complications
  • Addiction: Nicotine/Cigarettes capped until further notice
  • Aging: Related to above, cannot be used to represent youth
  • Mayfly Curse: Not an ideal format for its use
  • Sleepwalker: Causes a lot of problems in play with Mages
  • Fifth Degree: Only relevant to Technocrats, so irrelevant
  • Nephilim/Laham: Overpowered and does not fit well with our setting.

Books

  • Most of Gods & Monsters content is not allowed, especially those pertaining to familiars and other mythical beasties.
  • Book of the Fallen, just no.
  • Sorcerer (Revised) is not used in mortal creation, please use Numina magic instead. These can be found in Hunters Hunted II (Numina only) and the W20 Kinfolk book.

Restricted List

General

  • Willpower- Willpower above 7 is rare even among Mage. Even rarer among Werewolves and almost unheard of in Mortals. 10 is practically impossible! If you're shooting for 7 and above, please explain how you're so steely... More than a single 4 in Attributes or any 5's are unlikely to be approved, doubly so without ample explanation

Abilities

  • No Secondary abilities unless pre-approved by an ST

Backgrounds

  • Legend, Pastlives and Blessing for Mage follow similar rules to Spirit Heritage, Ancestor and Pure Breed for Garou, please explain how you got these and who are the important people in your lineage. Or in the case of Legend, what legend are you connected to.
  • Resources higher than 3 are extremely unlikely to be approved, especially without ample explanation and/or appropriate abilities (Finance, Law, etc)
  • Certification (especially for certain purposes) will be highly scrutinized and we reserve the right to request its removal or alteration.

Merits/Flaws

  • Enchanting Feature (Voice, Eyes, etc) is unlikely to be approved, especially for Mages who use it for their magic.
  • Stacking Social Merits are a no-no. Merits which promote meta-gaming and/or stacking negative difficulty modifiers will not be approved under most circumstances.
  • Stormwarden is fine, but we will be keeping a careful eye and will be more critical if this becomes common.
  • Permanent Paradox Flaw: Only one at creation

Current Caps

ALL

  • Common Addictions- e.g. Nicotine/Cigarettes/etc

Mage

  • Orphans/Hollow Ones
  • Kin-Mages

Shifters

  • Ahroun- currently capped
  • Fair Glabro (and equivalents)
  • Fera: Special Note
