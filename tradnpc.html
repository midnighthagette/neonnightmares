Angela

  • Name: Angela Rothstein
  • Deedname/Shadowname: אחות האמת, “Chvt Hmt,” Sister Truth
  • Apparent Age: Mid 20s
  • Breed: Mage.
  • Tribe/Tradition: Celestial Chorus
  • Auspice/Faction: Traditions
  • Visible Merits/Flaws: Detached Aura, App 4
  • General Disposition and/or Description: Yours is an ancient legacy, tracing back for generations upon generation. If your grandmother is to be believed, your family can be directly traced back to the line of David. You have your doubts. Your family has long ties to the city of Vegas though, both on the legitimate and criminal sides of its history. You were groomed to join your prominent uncle’s firm, the Rothstein Group and your uncle Shlomo Rothstein. Your Awakening changed that and you are now a proud and staunch defender of the Traditions, having traveled the globe as its Herald. Then you learned about your family’s dark past and are now ready to start cleaning house...
