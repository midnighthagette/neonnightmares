Neon Nightmares

Week of October 15th

Good whatever time it is when this comes out, folks! It’s me, Failover, with only the freshest of scoops. First up, Moot’s coming soon, so everybody get ready to brag and-slash-or complain. Remember, don’t bottle it up, that’s why we do this thing every month. Also, bring good snacks.

I’ve been asked to remind y’all that the Creepshow isn’t a safe zone, no matter how spooky you like it. Pick a different haunted house. Literally any other one. This has been a public service announcement.

Something’s stirring up the spirits of the Mojave—as of now, no clear idea on what, according to the Elders, but a whole herd of them pushed through Vegas out to the other side today, for no reason any of them stopped to explain. If anybody hears anything concrete we'd all sure like to know.

Week of October 8th

Once again it's your pal Failover, here with the latest news. 'fraid there's nothing good this week, folks. Surprising nobody, entering the Caern was a bad idea. Be on the watch for angry spirits. Theurges, get thee to the Five.

Closer to home, watch out for the "maternal healthcare" centers owned by one former Ruric Holt. They've got Wyrm taint all over them and are connected to tunnels beneath the city. Also, we have it on good authority they kidnap people. Move in groups and keep an eye on your loved ones. And if you're feeling some arson, remember, check for civilians and it's only a crime if you get caught.
