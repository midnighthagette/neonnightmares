Neon Nightmares

The Basics

  • Name: Neon Nightmares
  • Medium: Discord Play by Post
  • System: M20, W20 ((With some Revised as needed))
  • Setting: Las Vegas, NV (More Information Here)
  • Time Period: Modern (non-Covid)
  • Active Plot(s): Yes, each system has its own and there is also a metaplot which impacts everyone. You can find them here.
  • Summary: The Second Inquisition has risen and the world will never be the same. FIRSTLIGHT and other members of the Inquisition are on the hunt and have discovered the existence of the Garou and the Awakened (i.e Mages) and have added these newest threats to the Kindred already on their most wanted lists. Las Vegas has long been home to opportunistic Magi and Garou that largely ignored each other. However, now that agents of FIRSTLIGHT are on the hunt, no one is safe. This has led to a new and very tenuous alliance of the Traditions, the Torchbearers (a modified version of the Disparate Council), and the Garou against their common foe. Unfortunately, long held biases and ancient enemies are nipping at their heels and threatening to tear down the one bulwark they have against the Inquisition.   • Playable Groups:
    • Mortals: Shifter Kin, Sorcerers, Psychics
    • Mage: Traditions & Torchbearers (A modified and expanded form of the Disparate Council including defectors from both Traditions and Technocracy)
    • Orpheans: Skimmers and Sleepers
    • Shifters: Garou (both Urban & Rural identities), Fera
