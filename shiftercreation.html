Neon Nightmares

The Sheet

Our Shifter sheet can be found [Here].

Step 1: Concept

  • Species: What Changing Breed are you making? If you are making anything other than a Garou, please consult the document [here], for more information.
  • Please note: All Fera should be assumed to be part of the The Dutchman Caern unless you get prior approval from an ST, this need not be a strong connection but they should be known/recognized as a member of the Dutchman. Also, please submit a one-sentence Concept Summary to the "#Fera-Concept-Brainstorming channel before you make a character so STs can confirm your concept fits the site and does not conflict with our rules/restrictions.
  • Tribe: Any Tribe will be accepted. Ronin, Fera, and Skindancers are restricted and require approval from an ST . Black Spiral Dancers are not available for play. Tribal Weakness are not in play, but can be purchased as a 3 pt Flaw. Tribal background restrictions are still in place.
  • Breed: Homid and Metis are accepted, Lupus may be restricted if their numbers get too high in play. Remember that Lupus cannot buy certain abilities at creation and must use freebie points to buy them.
  • Auspice: Your Luna assigned position at birth- Ahourn (the Warrior), Galliard (the Lorekeeper), Philodox (the Lawkeeper), Theurge (the Mystic), and Ragabash (the Questioner)
  • Concept: This is a 2-4 word summary of what your character is all about.

Step 2: Attributes

  • Werewolves begin with 1 free point in each attribute.
  • They may then distribute their given points in the following categories: Physical, Social, and Mental. The primary category will get 7 points, the secondary category will get 5 points, and the tertiary category gets 3.
  • You may take a specialty in any attribute, but there are only mechanical benefits if the given stat is 4 or above (which any character with more than one 4 or any 5 will be heavily scrutinized).

Step 3: Abilities

  • Werewolves do not get any free points in Abilities
  • They may distribute their given points in the following categories: Talents - which are innate abilities anyone can refine, Skills - which are abilities which generally need at least some basic training from others, and Knowledges - which are abilities which require education and/or experience to possess.
  • The primary category gets 13 points, the secondary gets 9 points, and the tertiary category gets 5 points to distribute abilities in that category.
  • Your Ritual Knowledge dictates how high a level of Rites you can buy as a background. (2 in Rituals means you can only buy level one and two Rites)
  • Crafts/Technology/Science require a specialty at any level, other abilities may have a specialty to represent a focus but will only receive mechanical benefits if the given stat is 4 or above (which any character with more than two 4s or any 5s will be heavily scrutinized).
  • Note that your character may (but is not required) to take an additional language beyond their native tongue for each dot in the Academics ability. Additional languages cost freebie points. Additional languages cost freebie points. If you wish to purchase additional languages beyond your Academics points, you may also purchase the 1pt Merit "Language" for each and every additional language.
  • We generally do not use "Secondary" abilities (Jetpack or Teaching, for example) on this site. Consult with an ST if you have questions about secondary abilities.

Step 4: Advantages

  • Your character has 5 background points to spend at creation.
  • We do not allow PCs to have any background trait above 5. We also have house rules directly relevant to backgrounds; these can be found [here].
  • Please remember your Tribal Background restrictions.
  • Gnosis is set to Breed, Rage to Auspice and Willpower from Tribe. If you have any questions please contact an ST.
  • Pick one Level One Gift from Tribe, Breed and Auspice

Step 5: Finishing Touches

  • You have 25 Freebie points which can be spent to increase any one trait of the following categories for its stated cost.
    • Attributes: 5 freebies
    • Abilities: 2 freebies
    • Backgrounds: 1 freebie
    • Rage: 1 freebie
    • Willpower: 1 freebie
    • Gnosis: 2 freebies
    • Gifts: 7 freebies
  • You may purchase up to 7 points worth of Merits with your freebies if you so choose.
  • You may “buyback” up to 7 points worth of freebies in Flaws if you so choose.
  • Please consult the Merit/Flaw banned and restricted list before submitting your character. This list can be found [here].
