Neon Nightmares

General Notes

Rage

  • Players do not get any automatic Rage unless told by ST. Never assume!
  • Players can never spend more than half your Rage pool in one round (rounding up)

Soaking

  • Shifters can “soak” Agg Damage from “Tooth and Claw” from supernaturals, Fire, and (non-Wyrm) Toxic Waste. This applies unless specifically overwritten by a book.
  • Shifters CANNOT soak Agg Damage from Sphere Magic which attack patterns, (some) Spiritual attacks, and Silver/Gold (except in breed form, as appropriate).
  • Mages can use (appropriate) Sphere Magick to soak ONE type of Agg at a time (cannot be vague: “Energy” is too broad, “Fire” is acceptable).
  • Willpower Points CANNOT be used on soak (or damage) rolls. No exceptions.

Other

  • After taking your actions, ALWAYS flavor post in the main RP channel for the scene to show what you did (i.e. “Show your work”), but roll first to keep things moving.
  • +/- 3 diff is the cap, anything affecting diff cannot increase or decrease 3 past initial difficulty.
  • Difficulty is 6 for actions unless told otherwise by an ST or the Book (excluding Magic).

Step-By-Step Guide

Combat Rounds

  1. Roll Initiative using the diceroller. Current command is "/mi X" (where "X" is your Wits+Dex, form bonuses apply).
  2. Turns start at the top of the list and work downward. No declaration rounds.
  3. Players may take a Reflexive Action and either an Automatic or Full Action per round without penalty.
  4. Rage Turns go AFTER last on Init chart, in order of initiative
  5. 2m cap per turn to claim your actions, if that is missed, you lose your turn until the next round).

Classes of Actions

Reflexive Actions

“Free” actions. These require no roll and can be done in addition to Automatic or Full Actions with no penalty.

  • Move down the Init chart (per round)
  • Spending Rage
  • Shifter Healing
  • Reverting to Breed Form
  • First dodge of each round

Automatic Actions

No roll necessary, but takes a turn and causes dice pool penalties if paired with Full Actions.

  • Move
  • Returning to ready position after a knockdown attack.
  • Speaking (beyond single words or short phrases).
  • Ready Weapons

Full Actions

  • Attacks, including combat/martial arts maneuvers
  • Defending (excluding first dodge)
  • Casting Magical Effects
  • Most Gifts
  • Shapeshifting (excluding to breed form)
  • Most actions not listed here are also Full Actions
