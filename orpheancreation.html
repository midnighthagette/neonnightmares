Neon Nightmares

The Sheet

Our Orpheus sheet can be found [Here].

Step 1: Concept

  • Affiliation: All PC Orpheans are members of the Orpheus Group, a semi-independent splinter group of the USDOPA-SN division.
  • Nature / Demeanor - Please only use archetypes from the books. Please take note that some Shades REQUIRE certain natures.
  • Role: Any Role from any Orpheus book is acceptable (within reason). The creation rules of the Role is a SUGGESTION and needs not be an exact guide. Start with the Medium Variation (the bonuses from this MUST follow the exact wording if exact, if vague then you may spend those points as you wish).
  • Shade: What type of spirit are you when you project? This is partially determined by your Nature (so keep that in mind when creating!).
  • Lament: Are you are Skimmer who can project at will? Or are you a Sleeper, who must enter a medical ‘death’ in a lab or facility?
  • Concept: This is a 2-4 word summary of who/what your character is...

Step 2: Attributes

  • Orpheans begin with 1 default point in each attribute.
  • They may then distribute their given points in the following categories: Physical, Social, and Mental.
  • They may then distribute their given points in the following categories: Physical, Social, and Mental. The primary category will get 6 points, the secondary category will get 4 points, and the tertiary category gets 3. Roles give guidelines for this distribution, but they are not required.
  • You may take a specialty in any attribute, but there are only mechanical benefits if the given stat is 4 or above (which any character with more than one 4 or any 5 will be heavily scrutinized)

Step 3: Abilities

  • Orpheans do not get any default points in Abilities
  • They may distribute their given points in the following categories: Talents - which are innate abilities anyone can refine, Skills - which are abilities which generally need at least some basic training from others, and Knowledges - which are abilities which require education and/or experience to possess.
  • The primary category gets 11 points, the secondary gets 7 points, and the tertiary category gets 4 points to distribute abilities in that category. Again, Role provides guidelines for this but need not be followed.
  • Crafts/Technology/Esoterica/Science require a specialty at any level, other abilities may have a specialty to represent a focus but will only receive mechanical benefits if the given stat is 4 or above (which any character with more than two 4s or any 5s will be heavily scrutinized).
  • Note that your character may (but is not required) to take an additional language beyond their native tongue for each dot in the Academics ability. Additional languages cost freebie points. If you wish to purchase additional languages beyond your Academics points, you may also purchase the 1pt Merit "Language" for each and every additional language.
  • We do not use the Linguistics ability on this site.
  • We generally do not use "Secondary" abilities (Jetpack or Teaching, for example) on this site. Consult with an ST if you have questions about secondary abiilities.

Step 4: Advantages

  • Your character has 5 background points to spend at creation.
  • We do not allow PCs to have any background trait above 5. We also have house rules directly relevant to backgrounds; these can be found [here].
  • No venue specific backgrounds without express ST permission (e.g. even kinfolk or acolytes can’t take Totem).
  • You receive one Shade Horror Automatically, record that
  • You must choose 3 Stains (note: these are *not* always on and must be activated)
  • Derived Stats: Vitality, Willpower, and Spite are all dependent on a combination of Nature and Shade.

Step 5: Finishing Touches

  • You may list your character’s known Rotes (i.e. spells). You may take as many rotes as double your character’s Arete at creation for “free.”
  • You have 15 Freebie points which can be spent to increase any one trait of the following categories for its stated cost.
    • Attributes: 5 freebies
    • Abilities: 2 freebies
    • Backgrounds: 1 freebie
    • Willpower: 1 freebie
    • Shade Horror: 7 freebies
    • Non-Shade Horror: 10 freebies (keep in mind: each Shade has “banned” Horrors)
    • Vitality: 5 freebies
  • You may purchase up to 7 points worth of Merits with your freebies if you so choose.
  • You may “buyback” up to 7 points worth of freebies in Flaws if you so choose.
  • Please consult the Merit/Flaw banned and restricted list before submitting your character. This list can be found [here].
