Neon Nightmares

Percy

  • Name: Special Agent Percival Belmont
  • Deedname/Shadowname: Percy
  • Apparent Age: Mid 40s
  • Breed: Mortal
  • Tribe/Tradition: FBI
  • Auspice/Faction: FIRSTLIGHT
  • Visible Merits/Flaws: Lame Left Leg
  • General Disposition and/or Description: He prescribes to numerous conspiracy theories, ranging from simple aliens to elaborate shadow government conspiracies which extend from top to bottom. Partially as a result, he refuses to eat any food that he did not prepare himself, and he rarely trusts any thing offered to him, food or otherwise. He also has a strange paternalistic nature, likely stemming from him losing contact with his son, and he tends to “adopt” some individuals, who are the few people he truly trusts.

Virginia

  • Name: Virginia Mathers
  • Deedname/Shadowname: N/A
  • Apparent Age: Late 20s
  • Breed: Vampire
  • Tribe/Tradition: Lasombra
  • Auspice/Faction: Anarchs
  • Visible Merits/Flaws: Casts no reflection
  • General Disposition and/or Description: Virginia has always existed with only one purpose in mind, to survive and to thrive. Organization and structure can be useful tools, but often chafe and complicate life. As such she uses others as she sees fit and ignores any other trapping of society that interferes with her plans. As such, she lurks at the edges of the world, clinging to the shadows that surround the forgotten peripheries, and watching patiently. Live life as a predator, remain free and solitary, do what must be done to thrive and do not waste time on pleasantries and other such nonsense. Above all, survive.
