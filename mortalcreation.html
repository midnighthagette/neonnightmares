Neon Nightmares

The Sheet

Our Mage sheet can be found [Here].

Step 1: Concept

  • Affiliation: This is where you’d list the organization you’re character works with. Examples could include: Mage Traditions, Werewolf Tribes, First Light, Organized Crime, or even “None” for independent Mortals
  • Sect: This is only relevant if Affiliation needs to be clarified further.
  • Concept: This is a 2-4 word summary of what your character is all about.

Step 2: Attributes

  • Mortals begin with 1 free point in each attribute.
  • They may then distribute their given points in the following categories: Physical, Social, and Mental.
  • The primary category will get 6 points, the secondary category will get 4 points, and the tertiary category gets 3.
  • You may take a specialty in any attribute, but there are only mechanical benefits if the given stat is 4 or above (which any character with more than one 4 or any 5 will be heavily scrutinized).

Step 3: Abilities

  • Mortals do not get any free points in Abilities
  • They may distribute their given points in the following categories: Talents - which are innate abilities anyone can refine, Skills - which are abilities which generally need at least some basic training from others, and Knowledges - which are abilities which require education and/or experience to possess.
  • The primary category gets 11 points, the secondary gets 7 points, and the tertiary category gets 4 points to distribute abilities in that category.
  • Crafts/Technology/Science require a specialty at any level, other abilities may have a specialty to represent a focus but will only receive mechanical benefits if the given stat is 4 or above (which any character with more than two 4s or any 5s will be heavily scrutinized).
  • Note that your character may (but is not required) to take an additional language beyond their native tongue for each dot in the Academics ability. Additional languages cost freebie points. Additional languages cost freebie points. If you wish to purchase additional languages beyond your Academics points, you may also purchase the 1pt Merit "Language" for each and every additional language.
  • We generally do not use “Secondary” abilities (Jetpack or Teaching, for example) on this site. Consult an ST if you have questions about secondary abilities.

Step 4: Advantages

  • Your character has 5 background points to spend at creation.
  • We do not allow PCs to have any background trait above 5. We also have house rules directly relevant to backgrounds; these can be found [here].
  • No venue specific backgrounds (e.g. Fetish, Sanctum, etc) without express ST permission (this includes kinfolk and sorcerers).

Step 5: Finishing Touches

  • You have 26 Freebie points which can be spent to increase any one trait of the following categories for its stated cost.
    • Attributes: 5 freebies
    • Abilities: 2 freebies
    • Backgrounds: 1 freebie
    • Willpower: 2 freebie
    • Numina: 7 freebies
  • Numina include sorcery, psychic phenomena, Kinfolk Gifts, and True Faith. Please speak with an ST if you wish to spend points to purchase Numina for your Mortal
  • You may purchase up to 7 points worth of Merits with your freebies if you so choose.
  • You may “buyback” up to 7 points worth of freebies in Flaws if you so choose.
  • Please consult the Merit/Flaw banned and restricted list before submitting your character. This list can be found [here].
